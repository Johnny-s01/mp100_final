Little Island

Luna Park

Things to know

  • 🌷 Little Island is an artificial island supported by 132 tulip-shaped concrete pots, creating a distinctive floating appearance above the Hudson River.
  • 🌸🌳 The park boasts over 350 species of flowers, trees, and shrubs, meticulously arranged to provide a vibrant and ever-changing landscape throughout the seasons.
  • 🎭 Little Island features a 687-seat amphitheater and hosts a variety of performances, including music, dance, and theater.
  • 🎟️ Many events are free or low-cost, with ticket prices capped at $25.
  • 📍 Little Island is located at: Pier 55 in Hudson River Park, New York, NY 10014, accessible via pedestrian bridges at West 13th and 14th Streets in Manhattan's Meatpacking District. The park is situated on the Hudson River, offering scenic views and a tranquil escape from the city's hustle and bustle.
