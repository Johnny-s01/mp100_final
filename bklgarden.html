Things to know

  • 🌿 Founded in 1910, the garden has been a green sanctuary in the heart of Brooklyn for more than 100 years.
  • 🌸 It hosts one of the most celebrated cherry blossom festivals in the U.S. called Sakura Matsuri, usually in late April, but the trees remain beautiful throughout early summer.
  • 🎟️ General admission prices ranges between $16 for students and seniors and $22 for adults.
  • 📍The Brooklyn Botanic Garden is located at 990 Washington Avenue, Brooklyn, NY 11225, situated near the intersection of Eastern Parkway and Washington Avenue in the Prospect Heights neighborhood of Brooklyn.
