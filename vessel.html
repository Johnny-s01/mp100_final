Things to know

  • 🏙️ The Vessel is a striking 150-foot-tall. There are 154 interconnecting staircases, nearly 2,500 steps, and 80 landings in this British palace designed by Thomas Heatherwick.
  • 📆 The Vessel is open dailly from 10 a.m to 9 p.m.
  • 🎟️ General admission tickets are priced at $10, with flexible entry options available for $20.
  • 🗽 New York City residents can enjoy free access on Thursdays with an online reservation.
  • 📍The Vessel is located at 20 Hudson Yards, New York, NY 10001 , situated at the intersection of 10th Avenue and West 33rd Street in Manhattan’s Hudson Yards neighborhood.
